Evidence checklist

What to collect before a chargeback response.

The checklist should make the response easier to review, not longer. Each item should support the dispute reason, clarify the timeline, or show what is still missing.

  • Order facts: amount, product or service, date, customer identifier, and reason code.
  • Delivery or fulfillment proof: tracking, completion record, usage proof, or access history.
  • Customer communication: support messages, refund request, cancellation request, or confirmation.
  • Refund and policy status: whether a refund was issued, denied, pending, or not requested.
  • Timeline: purchase, fulfillment, support contact, refund activity, and dispute notice.

Redaction first

What not to send.

Send only safe facts

  • Platform, order number, deadline, dispute reason, and timeline
  • Relevant screenshots or notes with sensitive details removed
  • What you are unsure about before submission

Do not send

  • Passwords, login links, app access, API keys, or tokens
  • Full card numbers, bank details, tax IDs, or raw identity documents
  • Unredacted sensitive personal information
