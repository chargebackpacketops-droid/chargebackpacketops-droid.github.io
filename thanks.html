Privacy Notice

Last updated: May 21, 2026

RebutKit collects the information you choose to submit through the sample request form or direct contact. This may include your email address, commerce platform, dispute context, deadline, timeline, and redacted evidence notes.

We use submitted information to review your request, respond by email, prepare manual evidence-organization guidance, and improve the workflow. Do not send account credentials, API keys, payment card data, or unredacted customer personal information.
