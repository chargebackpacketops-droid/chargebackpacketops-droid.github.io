The real issue

A delivered-order dispute can still fail if proof is scattered.

In a product-not-received or friendly-fraud case, tracking alone may not tell the whole story. The hard part is turning order, delivery, customer-message, policy, and refund facts into one packet a reviewer can scan.

What usually goes wrong

  • The customer says the product was not received, but delivery proof sits in a separate carrier page.
  • Evidence is spread across Shopify, Stripe, email, support notes, carrier tools, and screenshots.
  • Tracking, policies, order details, customer messages, and refund status are not tied to the claim.

What the packet fixes

  • A short case summary for one opened dispute.
  • Proof grouped by claim, timeline, fulfillment, customer communication, policy, and refund status.
  • A review-ready packet you can inspect before anything is submitted.

For platform-specific preparation, review the Shopify chargeback response guide, Stripe dispute evidence guide, or the chargeback evidence checklist.

Data boundary

Send redacted notes, not account access.

Send

  • Dispute reason or category
  • Platform and order value
  • Submission deadline
  • Tracking status, delivery confirmation, and delivery-location match if available
  • Order page, customer messages, refund or replacement status, and store policy
  • A short timeline from order to dispute

Do not send

  • Passwords or login links
  • Full card numbers, tax IDs, or bank details
  • Unredacted customer names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses
  • Anything you do not want stored outside your own account

FAQ

Is this a Shopify app or Stripe app?

No. We do not require an app install, account login, API key, or carrier integration. You paste redacted business facts and receive a static document.

Do you submit the dispute for me?

No. You are the only person who can submit the evidence through your Shopify or Stripe dashboard. We provide the packet; you do the final click.

Is this legal advice?

No. We provide business documentation services based on payment platform requirements. We are not lawyers and do not provide legal representation.

Will this help me win?

No one can guarantee a chargeback outcome. The goal is to make your delivered-order response clearer, better organized, and specific to the claim before the bank or card issuer decides.

What if my deadline is soon?

Do not wait for us. You are responsible for your own submission deadlines. We suggest the free sample only if you have at least 2-3 business days left.
